# AnotherWorldsProblems
Hytale mod I created during the Hytale New Worlds Competition - April 2026.

Another World's Problem throws the player into a new world (without their permission) and gets tasked to help the village they appear in.

This mod is not intended to be played in Multiplayer or with other mods.

## Gameplay Instructions
1. Spawn at portal in village by generating a 'Generated' World type in Creative or a Exploration mode world with the mod enabled.
2. Walk forwards, which should trigger the short intro cutscene showing you the quest NPC Feran.
3. Walk up towards the Feran and interact to talk.
4. Your Quest HUD should update after interacting with them a few times.
5. You can explore the village for equipment and weapons
6. Follow the Quest HUD instructions, defeating monsters and then finding a "boss" - which is a Void Spawn enemy
7. Once you progress enough to return to the Spawn Village after deafeating the boss, your quest HUD will be updated to "Thanks for playing!" after talkinf to the Feran for the last time.

This mod contains a rudimentary custom cutscene system and questing system made from the ground up for this mod.

### Potential Issues

If you spawn away from the portal, make sure to trigger the intro cutscene by walking into it to spawn the quest NPC.

The boss spawn cutscene trigger may be covered in stone due to it not properly replacing blocks. You should be able to break the blocks around it to walk through.
